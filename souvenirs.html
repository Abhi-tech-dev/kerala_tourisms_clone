Souvenirs

Embark on a journey of discovery with Kerala's treasured souvenirs, each a testament to its rich cultural tapestry. From the sacred Yanmuri Pavithra Mothiram to the reflective Aranmula Kannadi, these items weave tradition into daily life. Illuminate your space with the Nilavilakku, a traditional lamp, or cherish craftsmanship with the intricate Uru Model. Store your valuables in the Netturpetti, a traditional jewel box, or adorn your surroundings with the majestic Nettippattom. Each piece promises a glimpse into Kerala's enduring heritage.
