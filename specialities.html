Explore the Specialties of Kerala

Discover Kerala, where tradition whispers through the peaks and waves along the coast. This portal is your compass to the enchanting realms of Responsible Tourism, Ayurveda's healing secrets, and culinary delights steeped in spice. Wander through vivid artforms, diverse wildlife, and serene hill stations. Embrace the monsoon's rhythm, the cascades of waterfalls, and the tranquillity of beaches. Explore village life, timeless museums, and the adventure of caravan tourism. Each link crafted below unfolds a chapter of Kerala, inviting you to experience its storied landscape and vibrant soul.

Village Life Experience

Experience authentic village life in Kerala, where traditional customs and simple pleasures depict rural charm.

Cuisine

Savor Kerala's culinary delights, from rich curries to seafood, are a testament to the state's diverse and flavorful gastronomy.

Artforms

Discover Kerala's artistic heritage through traditional dance, music, and crafts that tell stories of this culturally rich land.

Hill Stations

Escape to Kerala's hill stations, where cool mists and green landscapes offer a tranquil retreat from the tropical heat.

Wildlife

Venture into the wilds of Kerala. Witness exotic species in lush habitats from the Western Ghats to protected wildlife sanctuaries.

Monsoon

Experience the rejuvenating monsoon rains of Kerala that transform the landscape into a vibrant tapestry of lush greenery.

Ayurveda

Immerse yourself in Ayurveda in Kerala, the birthplace of this ancient healing science promoting wellness and vitality.

Waterfalls

Visit spectacular waterfalls in Kerala, where cascading waters create enchanting pools perfect for a refreshing dip.

Beaches

Relax on the serene beaches of Kerala, where golden sands meet the tranquil azure waters of the Arabian Sea.

Trekking and Safaris

For the adventure enthusiast and those with a head for heights, the trekking trails offer some of the best experiences. Eco Tourism Kerala offers many trekking packages.

Agri Tourism

Agritourism offers a unique blend of agriculture and tourism, inviting visitors to explore the captivating world of farms.

Caravan Tourism

Embrace adventure with caravan tourism in Kerala, offering a unique way to see the landscapes and live spontaneously.
